关于我们
这是一个由艺术家们建立的空间,旨在推广城市中的慢生活方式。在这里你能参与创造和分享艺术,体验轻松的创意氛围。无论您希望发掘自己的 创造力、与艺术零距离接触、或只想在忙碌生活中偷闲片刻,都能在这迷人的作坊里找到惊喜和激励。
About Us
A creative space founded by a group of artists who create, share and teach art. We promote slow-paced urban lifestyle through providing a creative atmosphere and relaxing experiences. Whether you want to discover the artist in you, to have close encounter with art, or to simply retreat from a moment of your busy life, you will be surprised and motivated by anything goes on at this charming studio.
